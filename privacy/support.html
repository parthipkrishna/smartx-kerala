About inPos App

inPos is a state-of-the-art Point of Sale (POS) Mobile Application developed by INNERIX TECHNOLOGIES LLP. The app is designed to streamline and simplify the billing and inventory management process for businesses, particularly small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs).

With inPos, businesses can efficiently process sales transactions, manage inventory, generate insightful reports, and gain valuable business insights, all from the convenience of their mobile devices.

Features of inPos App

  • Intuitive and User-friendly Interface
  • Real-time Inventory Tracking
  • Customizable Product Categories
  • Easy Order and Billing Management
  • Multiple Payment Options
  • Sales Reports and Analytics
  • Secure Data Backup and Restore
  • Multi-device Syncing
